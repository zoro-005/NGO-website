About The Organization

Our Work

Sankalap Ek Sewa Foundation is a devoted animal welfare NGO based in Meerut, India, committed to rescuing and rehabilitating injured, abandoned, and critically ill animals—including cows, bulls, monkeys, dogs, and more. Since its inception just a year ago, our foundation has already rescued hundreds of voiceless lives, offering them advanced medical care and helping them return safely to their natural or familiar environments.

Founded by lifelong animal advocates Mrs. Neetu Singh and Mr. Ajit Singh, our organization blends empathy with action. From providing emergency aid in cases like acid attacks, road and railway accidents, cancer, and tumors, to routine efforts such as sterilization and vaccination, our team works tirelessly every day to protect animals who cannot speak for themselves.

Our veterinary doctors and para-vet team are available round the clock, responding to urgent rescues and ensuring timely treatment. We also collaborate with trusted gaushalas to provide safe and healing spaces for rescued cattle in need of long-term recovery.

At Sankalap Ek Sewa Foundation, we believe that every life deserves dignity, care, and a chance to heal. Through unwavering commitment, we’re transforming how animal rescue and treatment is delivered—and inspiring communities to treat their fellow beings with compassion.

Sankalap Ek Sewa Foundation’s skilled team works daily in the field to provide emergency medical care to abandoned animals, with our doctors and para-vets always ready to respond in critical moments. From rescuing hundreds of voiceless beings and restoring their health, to ensuring safe relocation after recovery, our one-year journey has been defined by relentless service, compassion, and a deep belief that no life should be left behind.
